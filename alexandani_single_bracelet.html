    Young and Strong Expandable Wire Bangle - Russian Silver

    Availability: In stock

    $28.00
    SKU: CBD11YS03RS

    Faith | Hope | Determination

    The Young and Strong symbol is a tribute to remarkable women of courage. Wear this bangle to inspire empowerment, love, and healing through the power of positive thinking. Designed to wear alone, or to layer for a customized look, Alex and Ani's patented Expandable Wire Bangle is the most innovative concept in jewelry, allowing the wearer, with the slide of a hand, to adjust the bangle for a perfect fit. The Young and Strong Bangle is available in a Russian Gold and Russian Silver finish.

    20% of all Young and Strong sales will go directly to a unique program at New England's leading center, which provides optimal care, education and support for young women and their families as they cope with a breast cancer diagnosis, treatment options, and long-term survivorship.

    The Young & Strong Bangle honors the memory of Bridget Slotemaker, a young mother, wife, dear friend, and daughter. Celebrate her life and legacy with the adornment of this bangle.

    Bridget, a vibrant soul who left an impression on many and inspiration on all.

    For more information visit www.evening4bridget.org
